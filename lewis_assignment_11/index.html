Music

What is the one thing that everybody in the world has in common? It is the love of music. We are introduced as a baby to music when our mothers and fathers would sing us nursery rhymes. Music is also all over the world in every country and in every language. It would be hard to find anyone that does not music of some kind. It is one of the easiest ways to get us to come together, by singing a song together, whether in a classroom, in a church, at your job, or in a car. Music can help with many illnesses including depression by either taking your mind off your illness or the words of the song touching your heart. In this web site we are going to educate you a little about music.
